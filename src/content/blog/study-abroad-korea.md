A standalone News post lives entirely here on the site, with its own page.

Replace this with the real story of your time in Korea — what you studied, the
research or coursework, the people, and a few photos. Because this is a normal
post, it supports everything the Journal does: images, math, and code.

To turn an entry into a **pointer post** instead — one that only appears as a
spotlight on the timeline and links out to a full article elsewhere — add
`externalUrl` (off-site) or `linkTo` (another page on this site) to the
frontmatter and leave the body empty. See `news-pointer-example.md` for a
template.
