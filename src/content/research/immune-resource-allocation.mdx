## Overview

The immune response is, among other things, a resource-allocation problem. We
build a **systems-level PDE model** coupling innate and adaptive immune dynamics
to tissue-level resources, fit its parameters to biological data, and use
sensitivity analysis to show how **cytokine-regulated redistribution** controls
pathogen clearance and overall robustness.
